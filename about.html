About us

DAB is based on two banking and blockchain experts, Martin and Tarmo in Switzerland.

Martin has worked for the last 10 years for Credit Suisse, UBS and Man Investments in Switzerland and has been leading strategic intitiatives for years. His last 2.5 years initiative at Credit Suisse was 10+ mCHF Business Initiative for improving Sales Effectivness of Private Banking Relationship Managers through improved banking processes. Before financial sector he lead development and launch of four highly successful commercial products (three of them being artificial intelligence based). He is an EMBA, Dipl. Inf., Ms. Ec. and finished CFA Level III in 2010

Tarmo is Distinguished IT Architect (The Open Group) and banking expert. Peer to peer systems has been his passion for more than 10 years. He has 20 years experience in financial sector, from that 11 years as Senior IT-Architect in Credit Suisse Global Enterprise Architecture and 1 year as Chief Architect of most widespread Swiss Banking Platform. Tarmo has four Master degrees and one PhD degree. He has earned also CFA and CAIA designations.
